The Porsche 911

Author: Gloire Loya

"The 911 continues to be the perfect sports car for those who can afford one."
- Eric Stafford, Car and Driver.

Photo by Jessica Lynn Walker, Car and Driver


First debuted in September of 1964 by designer Ferdinand Butzi Porshce, this two door rear-engined beauty has marked its place in the world as not only one of the most beautiful cars but also one of the most iconic cars ever made, on and off the track.


Photo by Tim Scott, Sports Car Digest


The Porsche 911 has been dominating on and off the track for over half of a century. It's gone from being victorious in some of the most iconic races like 24 Hour Le Mans in France to taking to being one of the most popular vehicles among celebrities and anyone who can afford them.

It's proven to be such a versatile vehicle that Ferry Porsche Jr. described it as "the only car with which one can go on African Safari, run the 24 Hour Le Mans, head to the theatre or drive the streets of New York.>


Photo by Porsche
